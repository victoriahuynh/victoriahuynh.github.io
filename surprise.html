About

Thanks for considering me as your Director of Academic Support and for stopping by my website! To reiterate, here are my main goals if I am elected.

  • I want to help my peers be as informed and confident as possible about the classes they enroll in and the track they choose.
  • I want to make sure students of all tracks feel like their academic needs are represented equally in the iSchool.
  • I want to make sure students who are struggling in their classes know where they can turn to for help and that the environments where they can find help, like the TE Lab, are as inclusive and welcoming as possible.
  • Lastly, I want to make sure students are set up for success in terms of where to apply their knowledge professionally—many students don’t know how or why they should get involved in research at the iSchool, and internship recruiting season often begins in August/September, right when new iSchool juniors are just starting and might miss out.

Hit me up on social media if you have any questions or want to chat!

Regardless of whether you vote for me or not, make sure you vote in the Washington State Presidential Primary by March 10 if you're eligible! Exercise your civil rights!
